Lattitude Vs. Maximum Temperature

Unsurprisingly, the maximum temperature increases as you approach the Equater. We can also some what see the difference in seasons between the southern and Northern hemispheres. The southern hemisphere appears much warmer on average than the northern hemisphere, we can assume this is due to the tilt of the earth and is representative of the fact that the data was collected during the northern Hemispheres winter.
